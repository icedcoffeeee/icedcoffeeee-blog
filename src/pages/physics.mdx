# Physics
I’m currently a final-year physics undergraduate at [University of
Malaya](https://um.edu.my). I'm currently doing my final year project about
real-time infrared imaging for agriculture under the [Photonics Research
Center](https://prcum.um.edu.my).

<note>Written in Rust, by the way ;)</note>

![Image of lab](@/assets/lab.jpg)

I’ve always been fascinated with how robust the mathematical world is in its
systems and constructs, especially when applied in physics where any and every
phenomenon can be predicted as if magic was hidden in the runes of calculus or
code.

But the truth behind the universe is not just in these symbols, but rather the
approach, the thought, and the application. Understanding electromagnetic waves
meant nothing until the invention of radio.

<note>Fundamentals!</note>

Which is why I've always been passionate about teaching the intricacies behind
the gems of the universe, with a specific [YouTube
channel](https://youtube.com/@teh_ais) dedicated to that purpose. I'll always
believe that, in this era, physics and engineering becomes the lock and key in
innovation. Whoever wields them well steps foot in the next.
